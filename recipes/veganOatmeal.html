Vegan Oatmeal

Description

A recipe for an overnight vegan oatmeal made with plant-based milk and chia seeds.

Optionally decorated with fresh fruits and nuts.

Ingredients

  • a banana
  • oatmeal (3 tbsp.)
  • chia seeds (2 tbsp.)
  • plant-based milk (1 glass)
  • optionally: fruits and nuts

How it's made

  1. Mash banana into a container that you can store overnight in the fridge
  2. Add your oatmeal and chia seeds
  3. Fill the container with your milk of choice
  4. Store overnigth in a closed container
  5. Add your fruits and nuts the next day just before eating
  6. Enjoy :)
