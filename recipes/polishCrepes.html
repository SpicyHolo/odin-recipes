Vegan naleśniki (Polish crepes)

Description

A simple recipe for polish vegan preces using whole-grain flour.

Top them with ur favourite fruits, jams or whatever you have at home!

Ingredients

  • Whole-grain flour (70g)
  • All-purpose flour (30g)
  • Plant-based milk (250-300ml)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Vegetable oil (5ml)

How it's made

  1. Add both flours into a mixing bowl
  2. Add a pinch of salt and while mixing add your milk
  3. After adding milk, It should still have a liquid consistency, but a bit thick
  4. Add your vegetable oil to the batter and let it rest for at least 5 minutes before cooking
  5. Lightly coat your pan with a bit of oil and pour a thin layer of your batter and cook until the top side is done
  6. Flip the crepe and cook the other side a bit shorter
  7. Continue with the rest of the batter
