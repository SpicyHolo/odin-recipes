Chocolate Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Description

A simple healthy smoothie made with cocoa, pb and bananas!

Ingredients

  • a banana
  • Peanut butter (2 Tbsp.)
  • Cocoa powder (1 Tsp.
  • Milk (about a glass)

How it's made

  1. Throw all the ingridients into a blender
  2. Blend until smoothe
  3. Dilute with milk until you get theh consistency you like
  4. Enjoy
